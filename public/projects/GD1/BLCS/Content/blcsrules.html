BLCS

The ship is gone, lost to the abyss. You are trapped amidst the wreckage, scavenging floating pieces of wood to build the raft of your dreams. But the ocean is small, and there isn't enough timber for everyone.

The Objective

Be the last surviving pirate on the wreckage. If your raft is dismantled and you have nowhere to stand, you're shark bait.

The First Mate

The starting player is the person who can perform the best snake imitation. Turns proceed clockwise.

Course of a Round

1

Navigate the Raft (Move)

Move your pirate anywhere on your connected wreckage. You cannot move through or land on a space occupied by another player.

2

Secure the Wood (Rearrange)

Rearrange up to 3 blocks of your raft to any empty spaces bordering your current wreckage.

3

Scavenge the Tides (Draw)

Draw 1 card from the deck to conclude your turn.

The BLCS Cards

Play as many cards as you wish during your turn before you draw.
